Mukimo with grilled mutton recipe

This page is about making a delicious meal of mukimo x mutton stew. The recipe is meant for a serving of four pax.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of maize
  • 3-4 medium potatoes
  • a half kg of mutton
  • pumpkin leaves(or spinach)
  • cooking oil
  • 2 medium onions(sliced)
  • 2 garlic cloves(crushed)
  • 3-4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • salt
  • black pepper or mixed spices

Instructions

  1. Slice the potatoes and cut into small pieces.
  2. Boil the maize first, then add the potatoes and cook until tender.
  3. Add the pumpkin leaves in the last few minutes, drain then mash to make mukimo.
  4. Drain everything when soft, then mash together to make mukimo
  5. Heat cooking oil in a pan and fry onions until golden brown.
  6. Add the mashed mukimo to the onions and stir for a few minutes for flavor.
  7. Season the mutton with salt, garlic and spices
  8. Grill the mutton on medium heat, turning until evenly cooked and browned.
  9. Serve the grilled mutton together with mukimo.
